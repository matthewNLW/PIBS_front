Privacy Policy.

We believe your privacy is absolute. We do not use customer data to market to you, nor do we identify individual customers for marketing purposes.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Pawleys Island Beach Service, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers. Unlike many services, **we do not sell, trade, rent, or use your data for marketing or customer profiling**. Your personal information is utilized strictly to facilitate order processing, equipment reservation, and delivery logistics.

Information We Collect

To process your rental reservations and ensure accurate delivery, we collect:

  • Contact Details (Name, Phone Number, and Email Address).
  • Delivery Address (Your vacation rental property, beach access location, or home address on the island).
  • Rental Preferences (Dates, durations, and details of the equipment reserved).

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used exclusively to:

  • Fulfill your rental booking through our booking system, **Booqable**.
  • Coordinate and execute the delivery and pickup of your beach gear.
  • Process secure payments and verify identity for safety purposes.

Note: Your data is never analyzed, aggregated, or processed for commercial ad targeting, nor is it stored for the purpose of tracking your web habits.

Third-Party Processors

We partner with select third parties to handle specific actions:

  • Booqable: Our inventory management software. It stores your name, order details, and delivery address to maintain rental schedules.
  • Stripe: Our payment processor. Stripe processes credit card transactions securely and directly. We do not store credit card details on our local servers.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our data practices or wish to review the information associated with your rentals, please contact us directly at info@pibeachservice.com or call us at (843) 237-4666.
